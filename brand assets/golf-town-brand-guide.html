A Queens institution — where real expertise beats the big-box every time.

This is the shop regulars check first. Not because it's the biggest, but because it's the best. Grip changes while you wait. Repairs done right. Staff who actually play the game and know what they're talking about.

04 — Logo System

Logo & Mark

The logo system consists of a circular monogram mark (the "GT" or flag icon) combined with the wordmark "Golf Town" in Cormorant with "Bayside" as the locator sub. The italic treatment on "Town" creates warmth and personality against the strong serif caps. The brass flag-pin icon is the standalone mark for favicon, embroidery, and small-format use.

Maintain clear space

Minimum buffer equal to the flag icon height on all sides. Never crowd the lockup against edges or other elements.

Approved colorways only

Brass-on-dark, green-on-linen, or brass-on-forest. Three lockups. Nothing else is approved.

Minimum size: 120px wide

Below 120px the wordmark detail is lost. Use the standalone flag-pin icon at small scales (favicon, badge).

Never distort or rotate

Do not stretch, skew, flip, or tilt. The balanced feel of the logotype is earned through proportion.

No effects or shadows

No drop shadows, glows, outlines, or gradient overlays. The logo is clean and flat — always.

No placement on photos directly

Always place over a solid brand-color shield. Never float the logo on a photography background without a backing.

05 — Voice & Tone

How We Sound

Expert Direct Honest Local Unpretentious Serious About Golf No-nonsense
Write Like This
  • 25 years on Bell Blvd. We know golf.
  • Bring us your clubs. We'll have them ready before you finish your coffee.
  • Japanese shafts. Major brands. Beat any franchise price.
  • Not sure what you need? 10 minutes with our Foresight2 will tell you exactly.
  • Grip changes while you wait. No appointment. No excuses.
  • Charlie will tell you straight — what fits your swing, what doesn't.
  • Every club on the wall is there because it's worth buying.
Not Like This
  • Elevate your game with our curated selection of premium equipment.
  • Experience the Golf Town difference — where passion meets performance.
  • Our team of dedicated associates is here to support your golfing journey.
  • We leverage industry-leading technology to optimize your fitting experience.
  • Unlock your true potential on the fairway with Golf Town Bayside.
  • We are committed to providing an unparalleled retail experience.
  • Explore our diverse portfolio of world-class golfing solutions.

The Golf Town voice is Charlie talking to a customer he's known for five years — knowledgeable, straight, and warm without being soft. No corporate golf-marketing language. No "elevate your game." Specifics beat generics every time: "Foresight2 simulator" beats "advanced fitting technology," "Japanese shafts" beats "premium international brands." When writing, ask: would Charlie say this on the shop floor? If not, rewrite it.
