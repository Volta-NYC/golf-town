01 — Our Story

A Queens Institution

Where real expertise beats the big-box every time.

Golf Town has been on Bell Blvd for over 25 years. Owner Charlie built it on a simple idea: give golfers genuine knowledge, honest prices, and personal service you'll never find at a franchise. He carries all major brands — including premium Japanese clubs most shops don't touch — and uses a Foresight2 simulator for custom fitting that's more sophisticated than anything the chain stores offer.

This is the shop regulars check first. Not because it's the biggest, but because it's the best. Grip changes while you wait. Repairs done right. Staff who actually play the game and know what they're talking about.
